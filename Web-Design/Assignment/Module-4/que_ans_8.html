8. What is the use of the background-position property?

It sets the starting position of a background image. By default, it is placed at the top-left corner of an element.

It has different values like you can set it at left top, left center, left bottom, left top, right top, right center, right bottom, center top, center bottom, etc.

You can give percantage to set the background image and also pixels.
