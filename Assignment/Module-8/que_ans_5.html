5. What is the difference between Bootstrap 4 and Bootstrap 5

Bootstrap 4 has 5 tier (xs, sm, md, lg, xl) & bootstrap 5 has 6 tier (xs, sm, md, lg, xl, xxl).

Bootstrap 4 has limited colors & 5 has extra colors added with the looks & various shades.

Bootstrap 4 has jquery & related plugins & jquery is removed & switched to vanilla JS in bootstrap 5.

Bootstrap 4 supports both IE 10 and 11 & bootstrap 5 doesn't support IE 10 & 11.

We can't modify utilities in bootstrap 4 & bootstrap 5 gave freedom to modify and also create our own utility.

Bootstrap 4 doesn't have its own SVG icons, we have to use font awesome for icons & 5 have its own icons.
